Research Interests

I am now focusing on the video generation and world modeling, also multimodal fusion of radar point cloud and camera images for object detection in the field of autonomous driving. My dream is to combine computer vision technology with robotics to make artificial intelligence more efficient and widely used in people's daily life. Through three years of research, I have explored several directions, including:

Image Processing

Blind Image Quality Assessment(BIQA): how to give an BIQA score that is closer to real human visual perception.

3D Vision

Radar & Image Fusion Detection: how to use multimodal fusion to improve the accuracy of autonomous driving object detection.

World Modeling

Build a Physical World Simulator: develop an interactive virtual world that accurately replicates the physical properties of the real world.

Video Generation

Video Foundation Model: design a foundational model with strong consistency and high-level capabilities to enhance the development of physical world simulators.
